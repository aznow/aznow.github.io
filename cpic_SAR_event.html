JOIN US FOR A SPECIAL EVENT
Hosted by Central Phoenix-Inez Casiano NOW
 

STAND AGAINST RACISM
PRESENTED BY CENTRAL PHOENIX-INEZ CASIANO NOW

A part of EMPOWER HOUR MAY 19, 2018 6PM to 8PM
Changing Hands Bookstore, 300 West Camelback Road, Phoenix, AZ 

Join us as we get together to take part in the YWCA Stand Against Racism national event and participate in a facilitated discussion about how racism impacts our lives and what we can do about it.

"When community members gather to identify issues that directly impact them, they can use their collective power to create change in their community." YWCA

The first 5 people to arrive get a coupon for a free drink. So come at 5:30 to mingle before the workshop starts!

Learn more about the campaign at the YWCA.
