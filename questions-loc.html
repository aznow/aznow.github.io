Clean Elections Candidates are those who have opted into the Clean Elections Funding program with the Arizona Secretary of State. Such candidates agree to adhere to certain rules of the Arizona Clean Elections Commission in return for receiving funding from the Citizens Clean Elections Fund. Clean Elections Candidates can collect $5 Qualifying Contributions during the qualifying period (August 1, 2017 - August 21, 2018).

Contributions to these canddates must meet the following additional criteria:

• Be signed and correctly dated

• Be from a contributor registered to vote on the date the contribution form is signed

• Be from a contributor registered to vote in the proper district on the date the contribution form is signed

For additional information, please reference https://www.azcleanelections.gov/run-for-office/how-clean-funding-works
