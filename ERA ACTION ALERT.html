Tell Burges & Farnsworth to SCHEDULE THE ERA


Despite repeated requests and years of obstruction, the chairs of both the Senate Judiciary and House Judiciary committees have refused to schedule the ERA to be heard in committee. Public pressure is urgently needed.

Please either come to one the committee hearings in the next two weeks to protest (House Judiciary hearings are on Wednesday at 2:00 pm in HHR4, so Feb. 7 and 14. Senate Judiciary hearings are on Thursday at 9:00 a.m. in SHR109 so Feb. 8 and 15). Or speak up using the Request To Speak (RTS) app here: https://apps.azleg.gov/. A member of the ERA Task Force AZ will be in the building to hand out sashes for increased visibility when/if you make a public statement.

Otherwise, please sign up in advance on the RTS system to speak when the committee is hearing that day. When you are called to speak, ask the Chair (Eddie Farnsworth in House and Judy Burges in Senate) the following:

"Why haven't you scheduled the ERA to be heard in committee?"

                                      
                                    Burges                                              Farnsworth

Then tell them why they should: http://www.equalrightsamendment.org/why.htm

You could also simply stand silently during the entire hearing with your sashes on. You could chant if you are willing to be arrested.

So the level of protest is up to you, but please do as much as you can. If you can't do anything else, please call Farnsworth (602 926 5735) and Burges   (602 926 5861) and insist they schedule the ERA bills to be heard (if they are not scheduled, they will not be heard, voted on, etc..).
