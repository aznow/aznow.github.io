LEGISLATIVE ACTION ALERT – Featured Items

In spite of repeated requests, the chairs of both Senate Judiciary and House Judiciary refuse to schedule the ERA to be heard in committee. Obviously we need more and public pressure.

Please come to one or all of the committee hearings in the next two weeks to protest. House Judiciary hearings are on Wednesday at 2:00 pm in HHR4, so Feb. 7 and 14. Senate Judiciary hearings are on Thursday at 9:00 a.m. in SHR109 so Feb. 8 and 15. A member of the ERA Task Force Az will be there to hand out sashes to wear and make a public statement. For the more bold of you, please sign up in advance on the RTS system to speak on a bill the committee is hearing that day. When you are called to speak, ask the Chair (Farnsworth in House and Burges in Senate), "Why haven't you scheduled the ERA to be heard in committee?" And then tell them why they should. You won't get far because they will tell you to be quiet. Other actions you could do include taping your mouths with visible tape to show women are silenced by not being included in the Constitution, standing silently during the entire hearing with your sashes on, or chanting if you are willing to be arrested.  Or whatever creative ways you come up with!  The level of protest is up to you, but please do as much as you can.

If you can't do anything else, please call Farnsworth (602 926 5735) and Burges (602 926 5861) and insist they hear the ERA bills.
